The Two Cities

Paris and Washington have differences in age and Population, but most People who have visited the Cities say that they are very similar. Paris, The French City is over 2000 years old. The population of Paris is more than 2.500.000 People. Paris is the Political Center of the Country, Beside that The President of France lives in the heart of Paris, in the Elysees Palace. The French National Embassy meets in Paris, in the Palais Bourbon. L'Enfant is an engineer who designed Paris, the Capital of France, for this reason, many buildings and monuments are symmetrically located near one another. Paris is also a Site of magnificent monuments, Important works, fine museum and others, Every year, millions of tourists visit Paris to View its attractions.

Washington is younger than Paris, besides that the population is Smaller. Washington is less than 200 years Old. For the population, Washington has Just over 700.000 People. Washington is the political center of their country. The President of the United States lives in the White House, the heart of Washington. The City engineer was L'Enfant, a French man Who made the layout of the buildings and monument Look Symmetrical In one view. Washington is the Site of magnificent monuments, Important historical landmarks and Others that is why the City has millions of tourists from all parts of the world every year.
