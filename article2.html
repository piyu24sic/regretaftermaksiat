How to Play Well in eFootball™

eFootball™ is one of the best soccer games today, this game requires a lot of strategy, skill and calm. The high number of people playing this game, of course, is in line with the competition in the game. Likewise, many players there have difficulty in achieving victory. With this, I will give some tips to improve your game to achieve victory.

First, you have to determine your playing style. There are so many playing styles such as counterattack, ball possession, Out Wide, Long ball and others, you can adjust which one according to your playing style. Second, determine the coach according to the playing style that you have chosen, for example, Jose Mourinho with a counterattack playing style, and Pep Guardiola with a ball possession playing style. Third, determine the formation that you think is good for passing between players. There are so many formations that you can choose from such as 4-3-3, 4-4-2, and 5-4-1 and can even be customized according to your needs.

Fourth, Determine the Player according to the existing position by paying attention to the skills, abilities and playing style of the player according to his position. Fifth, this is a very important technique in the game, namely focus and calm, do not get frustrated easily if you are behind the score, stay focused on the strategy, and surely you will restore the condition.

By mastering these steps, you will improve your abilities over time, the most important thing is to remain patient and consistent in learning. Remember every match is our opportunity to learn to become great players
