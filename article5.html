The GOAT of Football

In the world of football there are two of the best players in history or commonly called the Greatest Of All Time (GOAT) in football, namely Lionel Messi and Crstiano Ronaldo. They have also been in the same league, such as the domestic league in Spain and even the UEFA Champions League. won many prestigious awards such as Ballon D'or, Domestic Championship, UEFA Champions League, Golden Boot, Best Player in Europe and others, because of this extraordinary talent and brilliant achievements they earned the nickname Greatest Of All Time (GOAT).

Apart from this, they have very different playing styles, Lionel Messi tends to be a playmaker who is tasked with organising attacks to create opportunities for his teammates as well as Cristiano Ronaldo, he acts as a goal machine who only focuses on goals in his team. In addition, they have their own special abilities Lionel Messi with his dribble that can pass a lot of opposing players so that it makes the opposing team difficult while Cristiano Ronaldo with the accuracy of hard shots that can break into the opponent's goal. in a match Lionel Messi relies more on technique and creativity to win the match, while Cristiano Ronaldo he favours a strong physique and his goal instinct. Lionel Messi's position tends to be inside because Messi is a playmaker who organises the attacking line for his team, but Cristiano Ronaldo is more advanced because he is a goal machine which forces him to be at the forefront. so the two have a striking difference Lionel Messi with his technique and creativity (magic) and Cristiano Ronaldo with his strength and goal instinct.

Both Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo have proven themselves to be the best players in the world who made history in the world of football. With their playing achievements and playing styles, they have inspired many people in the world, which is clear Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo have provided moments that will be remembered in the world of football.
